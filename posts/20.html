Polynetwork

Mathieu Ponton 2023-06 PHP
A project done with Maxime Antoine in 48h. Polynetwork is a social network for Polytech students.
Polynetwork allows students to create an account, add their socials, portefolio, GitHub, Linkedin and personnal website. They can, if they want, share the major they are in and their year of study.
The Goal behind this website is to allow students to easily create a network, and to allow recruiters to easily find students.
This project was made using PHP, HTML, CSS, JS and MySQL.
